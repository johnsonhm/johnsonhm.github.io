+20 Book Club

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Choose a book by browsing the FlipGrid and filling out the Book Club Choice Form.
  2. Read and record a FlipGrid for the beginning, middle, end, and word choice. If you are first, choose a question to answer from the Question List Document. If you do not know what section you are in, look at the Book Club Overview.
  3. Every Friday, fill out the Book Club Check-In Form.
  4. When you finish a book, take a Reading Counts Quiz and explore your book on TeachingBooks.net.

DISCUSSION:

  1. What happened in the section you read today?
  2. What is one adjective that describes today's reading?
  3. Which section of the book are you reading?
  4. Who finished a book today? Who passed a Reading Counts Quiz?
