Grading Policies


Grades are based on mastery of grade-level skills. Practice activities count for a completion and work ethic grade, while assessments make up the grades for each skill. Students complete a variety of assessments within a term including tests, projects, quizzes, and writing pieces. A student always has the opportunity to redo an assessment by filling out a "Request to Retest" form. I reserve the right to approve or deny requests based on student effort and evidence of additional practice.

Homework Help

Students should be reading at least twenty minutes each night for homework. Other than that, students may have work to finish that was not done in class. If your child is struggling to master certain skills, click on the links below to find specific resources that will help him or her practice at home (games, videos, worksheets). Also, you will find a description of exactly what each student will need to do to show mastery for each skill. If you are not sure which skills he or she is struggling with, you can check current grades online.
