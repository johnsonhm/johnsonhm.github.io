Book Club


Choose a book and read it. Check in each Friday. Record a FlipGrid reply for each section of your book (beginning, middle, end, and word choice). Ask and answer questions with details and text evidence. When you are finished, take a Reading Counts Quiz. Finally, check in with Mrs. Johnson and choose a new book.

Reading Debates


Pick up a copy of today's debate or find it online. Preview the vocabulary words by making note of the meaning of each bolded word. Read the text and take notes as a whole class, with a partner, or on your own. Go to Google Classroom to complete today's assignment.

top

Reading Poetry


Pick up today's poem or pull it up online. Read the poem a first time. Read the poem a second time and look for poetic elements. Read the poem a third time and look for deeper meaning. Go to Google Classroom and complete today's assignment.

top

Reader's Theater (Drama)


Pick up a copy of today's drama. Preview the vocabulary words by making note of the meaning of each bolded word. Choose parts and props. Practice reading your lines out loud. Ask for help with unknown words. As a class, read the play. Make sure you are practicing your fluency! Go to Google Classroom to complete today's assignment.
