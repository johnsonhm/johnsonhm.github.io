RPG

Arrange into teams. Make sure everyone has a copy of the assignment questions. Each team starts with 1000HP (health points) and 0XP (experience points). For each round, discuss and decide on a final answer for the questions in that particular section of the assignment. When you all agree, call over the teacher. For each correct answer, you will receive +1000XP. For each incorrect answer, your team must draw a damage card. If you answered more questions correctly than incorrectly, you won the round! Your team may draw a reward card. After resolving all the cards, your team may move onto the next round (next section of the assignment). When all teams have finished all the rounds, total up each team's XP. (Any remaining HP is converted into XP.) The team with the most XP wins! Note: If a team runs out of HP, the team receives -1000XP.
