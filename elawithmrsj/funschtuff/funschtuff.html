RPG

This role-playing game has teams answering questions in rounds. During each round, teams earn reward cards and experience points for correct answers. Teams must draw damage cards for incorrect answers. The ultimate goal is to be the team with the most experience points after all rounds.

top

Breakout EDU

Breakout EDU games teach critical thinking, teamwork and problem solving. You will receive clues that you have to figure out in order to open a variety of locks. The ultimate goal is to open the box.

top

Attack

This game starts with each team drawing an object or character (snowman, castle, monster, etc.) on a whiteboard. During each round, teams answer questions and then are randomly called on to share their answers. Correct answers allow the team to "attack" (erase) part of another team's drawing. The ultimate goal is to have the most complete drawing at the end of all the rounds.

top

Grudgeball

Grudgeball is a team game involving some basketball skills. Each team of 4-6 players begins with 10 X's. After answering a question as a team, one random student from each team is called to the front. Correct answers allow a team to erase X's from another team and a chance to shoot the ball. Shots that make it into the basket (either from the 2 point or 3 point line) allow the team to erase even more X's. The ultimate goal is to be the team with the most X's at the end.
