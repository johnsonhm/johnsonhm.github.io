Project 1: Digital Images

Introduction and Learning Objective

My image is going to be an interactive task list that will allow students to work independently through any given lesson or unit plan. When students work on their own, it allows them to progress at their own pace and provides the teacher with more time to work one on one with students. Although students are working independently, there will be certain tasks that will be completed collaboratively or as a whole class.

Given an interactive task list, the student will be able to: 1. Identify the learning target for the given lesson or unit. 2. Find and follow the directions to complete each task in order. 3. Successfully access the links and resources.
